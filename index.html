Gold's Standards

Prepare To Be Amazed

Check in etiquette

Advanced Reservation is required

Members will be asked to book a club visit in advance using the Gold's Gym Egypt app. Member can also book club visit through www.goldsgym.com

Face Covering

Members are requested to wear a mask when entering the club, when within 2 meters of others, and in all club common areas, including hallways, Rest Rooms, Cafe, and Supplement Shop.

Temperature Checks

Temperature checks and hand sanitizer use will be required prior to entering the club. Scanless check-in . Our front desk team will ve wearing face shields on top of face masks to allow safe and seamless entry to the club.

Towel Service

Towel service is not available due to the current situation. Also, bringing your own towel is not advisable as stated in guidelines of both Ministry of Health and Ministry of Youth and Sports.

Club Capacity

Advanced Reservation is required

Our clubs has maximum capacity to ensure smooth flow during your workout. By booking, your guranteed a 90 minute workout period. Members will be asked to leave our facilities to sanitize the whole workout area for the next reservation. You can book you club visit in advance using the Gold's Gym Egypt app. Member can also book club visit through www.goldsgym.com

Available time slots are 6AM, 8AM, 10AM, 12PM, 2PM, 3:30PM, 5:30PM and 7:30PM

Sanitizer Stations

Hand Sanitizers​

Members will be asked to use hand sanitizer when entering the club and frequently throughout their visit. Hand sanitizer will be available next to every workout station throughout the club.

Disinfectant Wipes

Members will be asked to use disinfectant wipes on equipment before and after use in addition to any Gold's Gym cleaning protocols. Disinfectant wipes will be available throughout the club.

TEMPORARILY ON PAUSE

Indoor Group Fitness

During the initial reopening period and as per local government requirements, indoor group fitness, GGX, will not be active. Outdoor GGX however, will be active

Amenities

During the initial reopening period and as per local government requirements, the following may be temporarily closed: steam rooms, saunas, spa facilities, pools, and Kids Club.

Give Each Other Some Space

Floor Markers and Signs

Members can give each other enough space, physical distancing cues will be marked in the workout area, at both Strength and Cardio Floors. Physical distancing signs will show which equipment is available for use, and signs will be placed throughout the Strength Floor to remind members to wipe down equipment before and after use and to practice physical distancing.

workout ETIQUETTE

Equipment Use​

To facilitate physical distancing, we will reduce equipment available for use. Select equipment will be unplugged and clearly marked to facilitate physical distancing. 30-minute time limits may be imposed on equipment during peak hours, if necessary.

Personal Training​

Members will be asked to practice physical distancing during Personal Training sessions. Trainers will remain 2 meters away, depending on local guidelines, from clients instructions will be done verbally.
